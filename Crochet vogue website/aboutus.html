CROCHET VOGUE

Welcome to CROCHET VOGUE, your number one source for all things crochet. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of hand made crochet clothing, with a focus on quality, affordability and uniqueness. Founded in 2016 by Irene Arhinma Dadzie, CROCHET VOGUE has come a long way from its beginnings on KNUST campus. When Miss Dadzie first started out, her passion for making handmade items drove her to start something profitable, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Ghana, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the clothing industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Irene Arhinma Dadzie
